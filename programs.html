Programs

We offer playgroup, nursery, pre-primary and daycare programs for 1.5 - 6 years.

Playgroup (1.5 - 2.5 yrs)

Play-based introduction to routines, socialization and sensory activities.

Nursery (2.5 - 3.5 yrs)

Foundational literacy & numeracy through play and stories.

Pre-Primary (3.5 - 6 yrs)

Structured early years learning that prepares for primary school.
