Our Curriculum & Programs

EYFS Curriculum – Learning Through Play. At St. Vincent's Preschool, we follow the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) — a UK-based framework that promotes holistic growth through play and exploration. Aligned with India's National Education Policy (NEP), it builds strong foundations in language, creativity, confidence, and social skills — helping every child shine.

Playgroup

Age: 1.5 – 3 Years

Core Objective: Social & Sensory Exploration.

  • Focus on separation comfort and secure attachments
  • Sensory play: sand, water, textures
  • Goal: routines & independent play

Nursery

Age: 2.5 – 3.5 Years

Core Objective: Foundation Literacy & Numeracy.

  • Pre-reading skills (phonics awareness)
  • Number recognition & counting
  • Goal: confident verbal communication

Pre-Primary 1 (PP1)

Age: 3.5 – 4.5 Years

Core Objective: Concept Application & Pre-Writing.

  • Phonemic awareness & sound recognition
  • Pre-writing strokes and letter formation
  • Goal: early writing & simple math

Pre-Primary 2 (UKG)

Age: 5 – 6 Years

Core Objective: Reading fluency, arithmetic basics & school readiness.

  • Advanced Reading & Writing
  • Mathematical Concepts
  • Problem Solving & Public Speaking

Day Care

Age: 1.5 Years Onwards

Core Objective: Safe, structured extended care for working parents.

  • Homework Assistance
  • Hobby Classes
  • Structured Play & Nap Time
