Our Curriculum & Programs

EYFS Curriculum – Learning Through Play. At St. Vincent's Preschool, we follow the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) — a UK-based framework that promotes holistic growth through play and exploration. Aligned with India's National Education Policy (NEP), it builds strong foundations in language, creativity, confidence, and social skills — helping every child shine.
