Our Curriculum & Programs

A foundation for lifelong learning through a play-based curriculum and caring teachers.

Playgroup

Target Age: 1.5 – 2.5 Years

Core Objective: Social & Sensory Exploration. Gentle introduction to school.

  • Separation comfort & secure attachments
  • Sensory play — sand, water, textures
  • Basic routines & independent play

Nursery

Target Age: 2.5 – 3.5 Years

Core Objective: Foundation literacy & numeracy through play.

  • Pre-reading & phonics awareness
  • Number recognition & counting
  • Confidence in group activities

Pre-Primary 1 (PP1)

Target Age: 3.5 – 4.5 Years

Core Objective: Pre-writing, phonemic awareness & concept application.

  • Letter formation & pre-writing strokes
  • Phonemic activities & group play
  • Early numeracy concepts

Pre-Primary 2 (PP2)

Target Age: 4.5 – 6 Years

Core Objective: Reading fluency, arithmetic basics & school readiness.

  • Reading fluency & comprehension
  • Basic addition & subtraction
  • Independent work & social readiness

Day Care

Target Age: 1.5 – 6 Years

Core Objective: Safe, structured extended care for working parents.

  • Meals & supervised rest
  • Structured activities & safety
  • Continuity of learning
