Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you accept?

We accept children between 1.5 to 6 years across Playgroup, Nursery and Pre-primary.

What is your curriculum?

Play-based learning with structured literacy and numeracy for older kids.

Do you provide meals?

Yes — hygienic snacks are provided; full day children bring packed lunches as per guidelines.
