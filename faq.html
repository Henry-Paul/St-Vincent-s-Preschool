Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the top questions parents ask us.

What ages do you accept?

We accept children from 1.5 to 6 years across Playgroup, Nursery and Pre-primary.

What is your curriculum?

Play-based curriculum focused on social, emotional and early literacy/numeracy development.

What are your fees and payment options?

Fees depend on program and timings; we accept monthly/quarterly/term payments. Contact us for current rates.

What are the timings and holiday schedule?

Standard hours: Mon-Fri 08:00–18:00. We publish an academic calendar for holidays and closures.

Do you provide meals?

We provide hygienic snacks. Full day children should bring packed lunches as per our nutrition guidelines.

What is the teacher-to-child ratio?

We maintain small groups with low teacher-to-child ratios to ensure personal attention and safety.

How do you handle illness and emergencies?

Staff are trained in first aid, we have clear protocols and immediate parent contact for any medical concerns.

Can parents visit during the day?

Yes — visits by appointment are welcome to maintain routine and minimize disruption to children.

How do I enrol my child?

Submit an enquiry or book a visit via our form. We will guide you through registration and document submission.

What safety & security measures do you have?

Secure premises, supervised drop-off/pick-up, and staff background checks. CCTV and access control are in place where possible.
