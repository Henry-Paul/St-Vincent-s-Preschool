Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the ideal age to start preschool?

Most children begin preschool between 1.5–3 years. We offer playgroup for 1.5+ with gentle settling.

2. What safety measures are provided?

CCTV, controlled entry, trained staff, sanitized classrooms and first-aid trained personnel.

3. What is the teacher-to-child ratio?

Playgroup 1:6, Nursery 1:8, Pre-Primary 1:10 — optimized for attention and care.

4. Do you provide meals?

We provide hygienic meals & snacks; allergies and preferences are carefully managed.

5. What curriculum do you follow?

Play-based, activity-driven learning focusing on cognitive, motor, language & social development.

6. Can parents visit during school hours?

Scheduled observations are allowed; minimal disturbance policy ensures children’s routine.

7. What are the school timings?

Playgroup & Nursery 9 AM–12 PM, Pre-Primary 9 AM–1 PM. Daycare extends to 7 PM.

8. How do you handle sickness?

Supervised resting area, parent notification, and access to immediate medical help when required.

9. Are activities included?

Storytelling, phonics, art, music, dance, early science, clay modelling, sensory learning.

10. How do I enrol my child?

Fill the enquiry form; our team will contact you for a tour, fee details and admission steps.
