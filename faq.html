Programs & Age Groups

We cater to children from 1.5 years to 6 years old. Our programs are:

  • Playgroup: 1.5 - 3 years
  • Nursery: 3 - 4 years
  • LKG: 4 - 5 years
  • UKG: 5 - 6 years
  • Day Care: 1.5+ years (flexible hours)

The ideal age to start preschool is between 2-3 years. At this age, children:

  • Are ready for social interaction with peers
  • Can communicate basic needs
  • Can follow simple instructions
  • Benefit from structured play and activities

However, we assess each child individually and provide guidance based on their readiness.

Our regular school hours are:

  • Playgroup: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Nursery: 9:00 AM - 12:30 PM
  • LKG: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • UKG: 9:00 AM - 1:30 PM
  • Day Care: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (flexible)

We also offer extended hours and early drop-off options for working parents.

Admission Process

The enrollment process is simple:

  1. Schedule a school visit (in-person or virtual)
  2. Meet with our principal and teachers
  3. Complete the application form
  4. Submit required documents
  5. Pay the admission fee
  6. Attend orientation session

You can start the process by contacting us or filling out our online inquiry form.

Required documents include:

  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • 2 passport-size photographs of the child
  • Immunization record
  • Parents' ID proof (Aadhar card, passport, or driver's license)
  • Address proof
  • Previous school report (if applicable)

Our admission policy ensures fairness and transparency:

  • Admissions are open throughout the year, subject to availability
  • We maintain age-appropriate class sizes
  • No discrimination based on gender, religion, or background
  • Siblings of current students get priority
  • We conduct an informal interaction with the child (not a test)

Safety & Hygiene

Safety is our top priority. Our measures include:

  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance in all areas
  • Secure entry/exit with access control
  • First-aid trained staff
  • Fire safety equipment and regular drills
  • Child-proof furniture and equipment
  • Regular safety audits
  • Staff background verification
  • Safe pick-up/drop-off procedures

We maintain strict hygiene protocols:

  • Daily sanitization of classrooms and toys
  • Regular deep cleaning of premises
  • Handwashing stations and sanitizers throughout
  • Clean, filtered drinking water
  • Hygienic toilet facilities cleaned multiple times daily
  • Staff training in hygiene practices
  • Sick child policy to prevent spread of illness

Curriculum & Teaching

We follow a child-centered, play-based approach that includes:

  • Montessori-inspired: Hands-on learning with specialized materials
  • Play-based: Learning through structured play and exploration
  • Theme-based: Monthly themes integrating all learning areas
  • Holistic development: Focus on cognitive, social, emotional, and physical growth
  • Individualized attention: Catering to each child's unique learning style

A typical day includes:

  • Circle Time: Greetings, calendar, weather, sharing
  • Academic Time: Age-appropriate literacy and numeracy
  • Creative Arts: Drawing, painting, crafts, music
  • Physical Play: Outdoor/indoor games and exercises
  • Story Time: Reading, storytelling, puppetry
  • Skill Development: Fine and gross motor activities
  • Snack/Lunch Break: Nutritious meals in a social setting
