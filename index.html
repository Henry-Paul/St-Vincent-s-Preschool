Our Curriculum & Programs

EYFS Curriculum – Learning Through Play. At St. Vincent's Preschool, we follow the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) — a UK-based framework that promotes holistic growth through play and exploration. Aligned with India's National Education Policy (NEP), it builds strong foundations in language, creativity, confidence, and social skills — helping every child shine.

1. Playgroup

Target Age: 1.5 – 2.5 Years

Core Objective: Social & Sensory Exploration. A gentle introduction to school life.

  • Focus on separation comfort and building secure attachments.
  • Activities include sensory play (sand, water, textures).
  • Goal: Mastering basic routines and independent play.

2. Nursery

Target Age: 2.5 – 3.5 Years

Core Objective: Foundation Literacy & Numeracy. Learning through fun, thematic activities.

  • Introduces basic pre-reading skills (phonics awareness).
  • Activities for number recognition (1-10) and basic counting.
  • Goal: Confident verbal communication and group engagement.

3. Pre-Primary 1 (PP1)

Target Age: 3.5 – 4.5 Years

Core Objective: Concept Application & Pre-Writing. Developing foundational academic skills.

  • Strengthens phonemic awareness and sound recognition.
  • Focus on pre-writing strokes and early letter formation.
  • Goal: Understanding simple math and mastering early writing skills.

4. Pre-Primary 2 (PP2)

Target Age: 4.5 – 6 Years

Core Objective: Primary School Readiness. Comprehensive skill development for Grade 1.

  • Focus on fluency in reading and basic arithmetic (addition/subtraction).
  • Emphasis on independent work habits and social maturity.
  • Goal: Smooth and confident transition into primary school.

5. Day Care

Target Age: 1.5 – 6 Years

Core Objective: Safe, Structured Extended Care for children of working parents.

  • Provides a seamless extension of the school day.
  • Includes supervised rest periods and nutritious meals/snacks.
  • Goal: Continuity of care in a productive, secure environment.
